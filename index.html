Research Areas

⚡
EV Drivetrain Tribology

EPSRC-funded investigation of aqueous lubrication for electric vehicle motor bearings and gears, addressing electrical current passage through tribological contacts.

🔬
Lubricant Additive Science

Molecular-level study of additive-additive interactions (MoDTC, ZDDP, Oleamide) using Sum Frequency Generation spectroscopy and Nano Infrared microscopy.

🧬
Bio-Tribology & Food Science

Frictional behaviour of plant-based foods, oral lubrication by saliva, and protein interactions at PDMS surfaces — bridging engineering and life sciences.

🧲
Polymer Composites & Surfaces

PEEK/PBI blends, carbon-fiber reinforced composites, and graphene-enhanced polyurethane bearings for high-performance, water-lubricated marine applications.

⚛️
Triboemission & Tribocharging

Novel methods to observe emission of ions, electrons, and photons from contact interfaces, and their role in lubricant boundary film formation.

🛢️
Base Oil Chemistry

Quantifying molecular structural changes in lubricating base oils during production using GC, FTIR, NMR spectroscopy, and bench-top hydrocracking.
